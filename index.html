About Me

I'm Aaron, and I take the time-intensive testing work off plugin developers' hands. You know the stuff: verifying your plugin works across buffer sizes, testing I/O configurations, checking compatibility with current DAW versions, validating behavior with multiple track counts, and hunting down edge cases that break user workflows.

I do the systematic testing you don't have time for, so you can focus on building features and improving your product. You get clear bug reports and compatibility documentation—no fluff, just the testing work that protects your reputation.
