About Me

I partner with music tech companies and independent music creators to accelerate their development cycle and guarantee product readiness through specialized QA. I specialize in diagnosing and isolating the root cause of bugs in plugins, presets, and sample packs so they can be confidently delivered to market.

My approach is simple yet rigorous: I test like a producer, but document like an engineer. My goal is to catch the critical issues that affect workflow and sound integrity before they ever reach your users.
